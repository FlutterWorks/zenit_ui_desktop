# Zenit UI (for Web)

Zenit UI is the [dahliaOS](https://dahliaos.io) design system. The Pangolin system shell, as well as the official apps, follow Zenit.
It was born as a fusion of, mainly, the Material and Fluent design systems, and includes components of both.

Despite its flat, modern influences, most evident in controls such as the [button](?path=/docs/components-button--primary),
the glass-like Acrylic material, and multiple shades of surface color, draw from Fluent and Material respectively.
